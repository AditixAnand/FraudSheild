Intelligent Scoring Algorithm

Multi-factor risk assessment:

  • Amount Risk: High transactions (₹100k+) add 30 points
  • Geographic Risk: High-risk countries (Russia, Nigeria) add 35 points
  • Merchant Risk: Crypto/Gambling merchants add 25 points
  • Device Risk: VPN (+15) or TOR (+40) detected
  • Behavioral Risk: Random baseline (0-20) + velocity checks
  • Final Score: 0-99 → Fraud (≥70) | Review (40-69) | Clear (<40)
